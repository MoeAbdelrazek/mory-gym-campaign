Market & Competitive Analysis

A strategic overview of Alexandria’s premium fitness market, highlighting growth trends, consumer behaviors, and Mory Gym’s positioning in the elite gym segment.

Alexandria Fitness Market Growth

The fitness market in Alexandria is projected to grow by 8% annually, with premium gym memberships increasing from 12,000 in 2023 to 17,000 by 2026.

Rise of Premium Classes

Group classes like HIIT, CrossFit, and functional training account for over 40% of new gym sign-ups, showing the growing demand for specialized fitness programs.

Consumer Preferences

Health-conscious Alexandrians aged 18–45 increasingly favor personalized training, nutrition guidance, and state-of-the-art gym facilities.

Competitive Landscape in Alexandria

Alexandria’s premium gym scene is vibrant with international chains and boutique studios. Mory Gym differentiates itself with elite trainers, diverse group classes, and holistic wellness programs.

Mory Gym's Strategic Positioning

Positioned at the intersection of modern fitness trends and holistic wellness, Mory Gym leverages Alexandria’s growing demand for elite health experiences to offer a premium membership experience.
