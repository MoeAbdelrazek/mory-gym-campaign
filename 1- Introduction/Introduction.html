Campaign Introduction

The Mory Gym campaign was designed to establish the brand as Alexandria’s elite fitness destination. By leveraging expert-led classes, wellness workshops, and a results-driven digital strategy, the 90-day campaign aimed to maximize membership growth, engagement, and ROI across multiple channels.

Primary Objective

Convert fitness enthusiasts and newcomers into active members through targeted offers, personalized workout plans, and engaging digital content.

Target Audience

Health-conscious Alexandrians aged 18–45 seeking premium fitness experiences, personalized training, and wellness guidance.

Key Channels

Social Media, Email/CRM, SEO & PPC, plus live gym events and workshops to drive trial classes and membership conversions.

Campaign Duration

10-step, 90-day campaign guiding potential members from awareness and trial sign-ups to full membership activation.
