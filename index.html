Campaign Performance Overview

Lessons Learned

  • Dynamic social media content significantly increased engagement among 18–35-year-old prospects.
  • Retargeting high-engagement website visitors converted over 60% of non-signups.
  • Email follow-ups with limited-time offers boosted conversion by 22%.

Recommendations for Next Campaign

  • Increase high-performing social ad budgets for Instagram and TikTok.
  • Launch more dynamic retargeting creatives for abandoned leads.
